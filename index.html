Privacy Policy for Spek Labs Automations

Last updated:

Spek Labs ("we", "our", "us") operates automation services including integrations with Facebook and other APIs through n8n and similar platforms. This page informs users ("you") about our policies regarding the collection, use, and handling of personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We use the collected data solely to enable Facebook-related automation workflows through n8n. No data is used for advertising, analytics, or shared with any external parties.

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is used internally only for operational purposes.

4. User Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by emailing us at support@speklabs.com. We will remove all associated automation data within 48 hours.
